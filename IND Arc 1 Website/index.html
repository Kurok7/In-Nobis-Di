In Nobis Di Emergence

Billionaire mogul Alex Kemper has decided to hold a fighting tournament to give people a chance for an opportunity to better their lives as long as they win and agree to a favor from him. One young lady, Eliza Azarius, participates in this tournament for personal reasons and wins. However, she learns the true reason for the tournament and soon her life is turned upside down. The story follows her journey to uncover the truth behind her history and to stop Alex's ambitions.

The Main Cast

  • Eliza Azarius
  • Aeni Silverlake
  • Jenine Zantaella

Over the course of the story, the ladies encounter more characters that guide them through their journey.
