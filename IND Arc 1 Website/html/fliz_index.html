Aeni Silverlake

About

This young woman is the oldest one of the trio and from the UK. She is a quiet and reserved lady who tries her best at anything she does. She is eager to know more people to conquer her shyness but her tragic childhood prevents her from doing so, especially around grown men. Once the abuse of her father ended, she was placed at an orphanage where she met her first true friend Jenine Zantaella. Despite Jenine's best efforts, Aeni was abused again by the adults of the orphanage. Jenine devised a plan to leave and go to America. They succeeded in landing but were separated. Soon, Aeni made it to Los Angeles where she was found by Eliza's father and was adopted into the Azarius household. There, she began her new life as Eliza's sister and best friend, even following her to Las Vegas. Her goal is to become a gymnast and teach everyone the wonders of gymnastics.

Heraldry

Aeni is the herald of Odin, said to be the wisest of the gods and extremely unique amongst the gods. She is a class 3, standard amongst Heralds. What sets her apart however is her potential to use the energy of Yggrasil, the only magic of its kind to rival even the Eternals in energy. Her capabilities are as follows:

  • Divine Ice (Cryo-kinesis)
  • Regeneration
  • Magic Manipulation
  • Frostbite

Though her capabilities are limited, she will gain more abilities at her disposal as she grows more proficient with her power.
