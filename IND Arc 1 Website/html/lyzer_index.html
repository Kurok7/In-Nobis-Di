Eliza Azarius

About

Eliza Azarius (O.G. name of Lyzer Spinel) is a book smart woman that is not afraid to go after what she wants. She keeps to herself most of the time and it's hard for her to establish an emotional connection with people (aside from her sister Fliz). She's pragmatic on a lot of issues, concerned about her own self interests and socially awkward. She enjoys reading and writing. She's detached from the world and its affairs unless it benefits her in some way. Her main goal is to figure out what happened to her father. Her dream is to open a non-profit for orphaned kids to help cope with their trauma. Over the course of the story, she learns the meaning of being a leader, opening up to strangers and a sense of duty.

Heraldry

Eliza is the herald of Zeus, the ruler of the gods. Due to this, she is in the exclusive Class 5 which is a title held for only one Heraldic family. Thanks to her class, she possesses the highest amount of Heraldic potential. She is capable of many feats with her powers, including the added benefit of using her lightning to sap the strength/durability of humans and inanimate objects.

    Skills

  • Divine Lightning (Electro-kinesis)
  • Regeneration
  • Amplification
  • Static Magnetism
  • Constitutional Degradation

Though her capabilities are limited, she will gain more abilities at her disposal as she grows more proficient with her power.
