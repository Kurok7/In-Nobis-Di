In Nobis Di Emergence

Billionaire mogul Alex Kemper has decided to hold a fighting tournament to give people a chance for an opportunity to better their lives. However, the true intention of the tournament is unknown to the participants. The story follows three young ladies taken on a journey to stop Alex's ambitions, but uncover secrets along the way that'll grant them power but will question their dedication to the task.

The Main Cast

  • Eliza Azarius
  • Aeni Silverlake
  • Jenine Zantaella
  • Akiko Hikari

Over the course of the story, the ladies encounter more characters that guide them through their journey.
