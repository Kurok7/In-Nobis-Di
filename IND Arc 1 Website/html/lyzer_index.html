Eliza Azarius

About

This young woman is an aspiring MMA contender from Los Angeles. She fights because she found out at a young age that her talent was fighting due to her never losing a match to her peers. She honed her skills from her master Kuro and developed her own style over the years to become an unstoppable force in the ring. She hopes to amass enough money to open her own studio and train women on defending themselves. Her goal is directly tied to the troubled past of her dear sister Aeni Silverlake. Eliza's past wasn't also peaches and cream as she had to deal with a father who abandoned her and Aeni after her mother died. She soon moved to Vegas to leave her past behind and start anew.

Heraldry

Eliza is the herald of Zeus, the ruler of the gods. Due to this, she is in the exclusive Class 5 which is a title held for only one Heraldic family. Thanks to her class, she possesses the highest amount of Heraldic potential. She is capable of many feats with her powers.

    Skills

  • Divine form of lightning (Electrokinesis)
  • Regeneration
  • Amplification
  • Static Magnetism

Though her capabilities are limited, she will gain more abilites at her disposal as she grows more proficient with her power.
